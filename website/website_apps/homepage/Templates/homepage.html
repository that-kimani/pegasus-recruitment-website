About Us
Established in 1982, Pegasus Recruiting is an agency with the sole aim of helping fresh graduates and talented minds find their way into the corporate world. We go beyond mere placement, offering personalized career guidance and mentorship to empower candidates in navigating the complexities of the modern job market.

Our extensive network of industry connections allows us to match individuals not only with job opportunities but also with environments where they can thrive and grow professionally. With a focus on fostering long-term success, we support candidates in honing their skills, building their confidence, and achieving their career aspirations.

Join us on the journey to unlock your full potential and soar to new heights in your
professional life.
